Unassailable Vertical Integration

Functioning as a critical regional node within the architecture of the Singapore-based Prima Group, CGE extends far beyond conventional commodity feed manufacturing. We have systematically engineered a fully vertically integrated agribusiness ecosystem whose operational footprint spans the entirety of the modern poultry value chain.

Our foundational architecture encompasses bulk grain transshipment, highly automated computerized feed milling, flock genetic breeding, commercial farming, and advanced downstream meat processing.

Biological Traceability via Movex ERP

To control the logistics of tracking millions of living organisms alongside volatile feed materials, CGE utilizes the highly advanced Movex Enterprise Resource Planning system.

  • Provides absolute end-to-end biological and chemical tracking.
  • Executes complete backward data tracing from point of sale to specific grandparent flock.
  • Serves as the central core of our national biosecurity and quarantine protocols.

Corporate Heritage & Operations

The foundation of Ceylon Grain Elevators is inextricably linked to Prima Limited, a multinational conglomerate founded in 1961 as the first flour mill in Singapore. Following the establishment of Prima Ceylon in 1977, Ceylon Grain Elevators PLC (CGE) was formally incorporated in May 1982 and listed as a public entity on the Colombo Stock Exchange in 1984.

Functioning as a critical regional node within the broader architecture of the Prima Group, CGE operates as the largest, most technologically advanced, and structurally complex integrated poultry and feed milling enterprise in the Sri Lankan market. We have actively pioneered deep vertical integration to insulate the domestic protein supply from external volatility.
